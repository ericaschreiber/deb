Deb partners with trailblazers who find themselves in repeating and recycling territories — which limits the possibilities for what they can create next. About Deb

"The deepest transformation begins when we stop protecting ourselves and remember who we are."

Deb works with trailblazers—founders, executives, coaches—who are successful but not yet free. Leaders whose drive has carried them far, and whose patterns are limiting what they can create next.

Her path hasn't been a straight line. She came to coaching through hospice nursing, where she learned to be fully present when it matters most.
