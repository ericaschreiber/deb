Discover the work

Deb partners with trailblazers who find themselves in repeating and recycling territories — which limits the possibilities for what they can create next. With relentless love and exacting attention, she helps them recover the heart of the seeker, the mind of the visionary, and the will of the warrior—so they can reckon with their power, make the next brave choice, and forge freedom.

About Deb

"The deepest transformation begins when we stop protecting ourselves and remember who we are."

She works with trailblazers—founders, executives, coaches—who are successful but not yet free. Leaders whose drive has carried them far, and whose patterns are limiting what they can create next.

Her path hasn't been a straight line. She came to coaching through hospice nursing, where she learned to be fully present when it matters most. She trained in earth-based wisdom with RainbowHawk and WindEagle of Ehama Institute. She was a coach and founding member of Conscious Leadership Group from 2017–2025.

Today, Deb coaches leaders, facilitates team trainings, leads Signal Forums and Intensives, supervises coaches, and speaks.
