About Deb

She has spent her life
learning how freedom
is made.

Deb Katz is an executive coach and partner to visionary leaders — founders, executives, coaches who are successful, but not yet free. She brings to her work more than two decades of coaching, executive facilitation, and team leadership, alongside a background in hospice nursing that taught her what it means to be fully present with another person at the edge of what matters most.

She leads Signal Forums, coaches one-on-one, facilitates teams and boards, and shows up as a balcony coach — stepping into the elevated observer's perspective to help leaders see what they can't see from the ground of their own doing.

lineage

Ehama Institute ↗

Deb had the great fortune of training in earth-based wisdom with RainbowHawk and WindEagle of Ehama Institute in the Santa Cruz mountains. This exploration of the interdependent web of All Life — of the unseen systems shaping how we see and move in the world — became a seminal homecoming. To her own true nature. To something feral and real beneath the noise of an identity.

This lineage runs beneath everything she does with clients: the attunement to systems invisible to the analytical mind, the trust in what can be sensed before it can be named, the capacity to hold a person in the fire without flinching.
