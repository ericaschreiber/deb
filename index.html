About Deb

"The deepest transformation begins when we stop protecting ourselves and remember who we are."

Deb works with trailblazers—founders, executives, coaches—who are successful but not yet free. Leaders whose drive has carried them far, and whose patterns are limiting what they can create next.

Her path hasn't been a straight line. She came to coaching through hospice nursing, where she learned to be fully present when it matters most. Deb trained in earth-based wisdom with RainbowHawk and WindEagle of Ehama Institute. She was a coach and founding member of Conscious Leadership Group from 2017–2025.

Today, Deb coaches leaders, facilitates team trainings, leads Signal Forums and Intensives, supervises coaches, and speaks.
